# AI Slop Guardian

The ultimate trust layer for open-source ecosystems.

## 🚀 The 10-Feature Evolution

Shield your repository from AI-generated noise with our 10 advanced defense mechanisms:

1. **🧠 AI Comprehension Challenges**: High-score PRs are met with technically specific, Claude-generated implementation questions. Authors must verify their understanding to merge.
2. **👻 Ghost Author Detection**: Detects sudden stylistic shifts in contributors by analyzing their previous 10 PRs against their current submission.
3. **🤖 Smart AI Reviews**: Automated, technically-deep reviews that identify bugs, quality issues, and slop patterns in real-time.
4. **🔌 VS Code Extension**: Shift left! Detect potential slop in the IDE before the `git push` happens.
5. **🔥 Burnout Analytics**: Visualizes the "tax" AI slop places on maintainers—tracking wasted hours and projected productivity loss.
6. **🧬 Code DNA Matching**: Cross-repo plagiarism detection tracks content patterns across the entire open-source network.
7. **🌊 Surge DDoS Protection**: Closes PRs automatically during high-volume bot attacks to protect CI/CD resources.
8. **📛 Trust Badges**: Dynamic repository badges showing the 30-day "Human-Verified" rate.
9. **🕸️ Contributor Trust Graph**: A social capital network that identifies clusters of trusted human contributors.
10. **🧩 Semantic Coherence**: Cross-file analysis ensuring PR titles, bodies, and code actually align semantically.
